Term Project Presentation

  • Come to MindScapes May 9 at 6:30pm

  • Bring your GameBoy and GameBoy cartridge, loaded with your game

  • If you have amplified speakers or a machine which plays GameBoy games with real GameBoy (not emulated) hardware and has a video out, please bring this too.

  • If your application is not self-explanatory, print out directions for how to use your game, and bring several copies of this with you to MindScapes.

  • At the event, show your game to pre-teenagers who are visiting MindScapes for their weekly Game Night.

  • Show-time is at 7pm

Purpose

This is a chance to show off your final project and to connect with youth who might use software but have never considered developing software. By completing this assignment you will:

  • have attended the MindScapes event from 7 to 9pm

  • shown your GameBoy application on your GameBoy to youth who are attending Game Night at MindScapes

  • have interacted with the youth. Answered the questions they asked etc.
  • taken a look at everybody else's applications in the class during the show.

I am hoping this will be fun and have the feeling of a party. Let's have a good time!
