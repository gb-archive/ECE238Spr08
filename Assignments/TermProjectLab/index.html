Term Project Lab

  • Post to the blog, explaining what you want to accomplish in the class labs on April 7 and 9.
  • Post due April 5, midnight

Background and Purpose

  • We are barely more than a month away from when the final project is due.
  • To help us all stay on track. Classes on April 7 and April 9 will be in the computer lab on the 3rd floor of Wallace Hall.
  • During class lab, you can work on your projects on the computers at the lab or on your own laptop.

    • The Wallace Hall computers do not have the assembler software installed on the machines. You must bring your own USB flash drive to assemble your code.

  • I will be at the lab to help you with any problems
  • In order to use this time efficiently, it is important that both you and I know what you want to accomplish. This way
    • you can be focused with your goals
    • I can be prepared to help you with the challenges you will come across.

Assignment Details

  • review your milestones for Phase I
  • think about what you need to accomplish the week of April 7 to accomplish your milestones
  • consider the challenges you face and what you might need help on.
  • write a post to the blog under the category "Project Lab." Include in your post what challenges you hope to tackle in the class labs.
    • More detail is better but give at least a few sentences outlining the challenge and the specifics of what it will take to overcome it.

  • as always, you are encouraged to comment on other people's work.
